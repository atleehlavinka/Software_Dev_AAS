FAQs

Do you provide Gluten-Free options?

Yes! We have three kinds of gluten-free bagels. Cinnamon Raisin, Everything, and Plain.

Can I order online?

You certainly can by downloading the DoorDash App, or giving us a call. We will have your order ready for pickup within 30 minutes of your call, or a later time of your choosing.

What if there's an ingredient that isn't available?

Please let us know! We love to add new bagels, cream cheeses, and bagel toppings.

If I want to compliment the staff, what's the best way to do so?

Emailing us is the best way to keep track of your praise. We use these emails to reward outstanding employees.
